1. Information We Collect

We only ask for the data needed to personalise your experience and sync securely across devices.

  • Account Information: Email address and authentication credentials when you create an account.
  • Mood Data: Your daily mood entries, selected emotions, and any notes you voluntarily provide.
  • Usage Data: High-level analytics about how the app is used (e.g. timestamps, feature engagement) so we can see what helps most.

2. How We Use Your Information

Data is used exclusively to deliver and improve Daily Pulse:

  • Powering your core journaling and mood tracking experience.
  • Syncing entries securely across devices that you sign in on.
  • Generating personal insights and visualisations about your emotional patterns.
  • Guiding design and product decisions so we can improve what matters.
  • Keeping you informed about significant product updates.

3. Data Storage & Security

We use industry-grade services and encryption to keep your entries safe at rest and in transit.

  • Firebase Authentication: Provides secure sign-in and account protection.
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores your mood data encrypted in the cloud for seamless sync.
  • Local Storage: Hive database keeps a private, offline copy on your device so you always have access.

4. Data Sharing

Your data is yours. We never sell or trade it. We only share data when:

  • You give explicit consent.
  • We are legally required to respond to a valid request.
  • It is necessary to protect our rights, investigate fraud, or ensure user safety.

5. Third-Party Services

Daily Pulse relies on a small, trusted set of services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and cloud data storage, each governed by their own privacy policies.

6. Your Rights

You stay in control. You can request to:

  • Access the personal data we store about you.
  • Correct any inaccurate account information.
  • Delete your account and associated data.
  • Export your mood history for personal records.
  • Opt out of non-essential analytics.

7. Children's Privacy

Daily Pulse is designed for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Data Retention

We store your data for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, we remove personal data within 30 days unless legal obligations require a longer period.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. When we do, we update the "Last updated" date and share a notice inside the app so you know what changed.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed or stored on servers located outside of your country. In those cases we rely on safeguards, including Standard Contractual Clauses, to protect your rights.

Need anything else?

Our team is happy to answer questions about this policy or help you manage your data. We reply to every message.
