FAQs

Is my data private?

Yes. Your mood data belongs to you. We use Firebase Auth and Firestore for secure sync, and you can export or delete data anytime.

Is there an offline mode?

Yes. Daily Pulse works offline using local storage. Your entries sync when you're back online.

Can I switch themes?

Absolutely. System theme is respected and you can choose Light or Dark manually in Settings.
